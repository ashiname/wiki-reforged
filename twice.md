# TWICE

TWICE is a south korean music group. It was formed in 2016 in the reality show Sixteen, by JYP Entertainment, their label until today. It consists in 9 members (Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Jihyo, Sana, Momo, Mina, Chaeyoung, Dahyun, Tzuyu).
