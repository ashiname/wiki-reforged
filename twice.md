<h1>TWICE</h1>

TWICE is a south korean music group. It was formed in 2016 in the reality show Sixteen, by JYP Entertainment, their label until today. It consists in 9 members (Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Jihyo, Sana, Momo, Mina, Chaeyoung, Dahyun, Tzuyu).

# Sixteen
Sixteen was a 10 episodes reality show where JYP Entertainment trainees competed to prove who deserved to be part of the new upcoming JYP girl group. They did a number of dynamics and competitions to show their abilities and personality traits, aiming to win and/or prove they were the ideal pick.
